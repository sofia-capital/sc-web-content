## What is Sofia Capital?

Sofia Capital is an open knowledge project about finance, created to combat misinformation and false investment gurus. Here I share, for free, my personal notes and learnings acquired through books and courses, with the aim of helping those who are starting from scratch. 

It is not a course nor professional advice, but a structured compilation of everything I have learned, and that I feel the need to share with you.
